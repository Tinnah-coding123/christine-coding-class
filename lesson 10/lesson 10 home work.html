traditional dishes from different tribes in Africa

  • matooke and groundnuts from the baganda tribe
  • sweetpotatoes for the basoga tribe
  • millet for the acholi
  • irishpotatoes for the bakiga
  • millet and plantain for the bagisu

Each tribe is identified by its main dish for example the Baganda – its Luwombo, Banyankole – Millet, Basoga – Cassava, Sabin – Matooke and Bagisu – Malewa, Itesots, Acholi, Langi, Karamojongs – Millet. The Basoga – Sweet potatoes, the Batooro – Millet, Bakiga – Irish potatoes.

Other common foods in Uganda include bananas, pineapple, cabbage, and spinach. Groundnuts are also a vital staple that are often turned into a sauce.
