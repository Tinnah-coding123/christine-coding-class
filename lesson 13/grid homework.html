baganda
matooke and groundnuts
Ugandan traditional meal with matoke steamed and served with luwombo, meat or groundnuts steamed in banana leaves.
basoga
sweetpotatoes and groudnuts
The most popular food eaten by the Basoga is sweet potatoes. They eat embooli n'envuluga, which translates to sweet potatoes and ground nut sauce. They steam, roast, or boil their sweet potatoes in a bid to eat them in a variety of ways.
